# Market + growth collaborator scope

## Market research checklist
- Survey 20 tutorial creators about current editing workflow and time cost.
- Interview 5 dev tutors and 5 educators on clip formats and publish friction.
- Track competitor pricing for clipping tools with free tiers.

## Landing page copy blocks
- Headline: "One tutorial upload. Multiple short-form clips."
- Subheadline: "SplitShorts creates vertical split-screen clips with captions and publish metadata."
- CTA: "Join beta" and "See sample outputs"

## Onboarding messaging
- Step 1: Upload one tutorial video with webcam overlay.
- Step 2: Pick webcam crop and caption style.
- Step 3: Download clips + copy publish pack.

## Beta recruiting plan
- Channels: Reddit creator communities, Indie Hackers, X dev creator circles.
- Message: Free early access in exchange for workflow feedback and sample clips.
- Goal: 30 active beta users in first month.

## Sample outputs plan
- Curate 12 clips from 4 niches.
- Show before/after with original timeline and resulting short.
- Include metadata examples for each target platform.
